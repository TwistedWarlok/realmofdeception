Better Skeletons!

The skeleton sprite that is included with RPG Maker MV is a little scary and a little cute. It might not be what game designers are looking for when they search for a skeleton. I've make some simple edits to clean it up a bit.

  • Removed the necklace and headdress.
  • Picked some new colors for the loincloth that made more sense for undead critters than pink.
  • The basic skull face looked a little to zen for me, so I gave it a slight snarl by furrowing the brow and adjusting the teeth.
  • Bonus: An alternate version with nice red glowies in the eye sockets .

Horny Better Skeletons!

These skeletons have wicked black horns, borrowed from the minotaur-looking guy on the monsters sheet.

Spoiler

 

Animated Specimen Tanks

I needed some specimen tanks for a map I was working on... so I created some using the metal industrial-looking column from the SF_Inside and SF_Outside tilesets.

  • Fourteen colors: primary, secondary, tertiary, black, and white.
  • Transparent and opaque fluids.
  • Bonus: empty specimen tank and without glass.
Spoiler

 

And here's a screen capture.

 

Animated Water Autotile Edits
I was working on a small map using the snow tiles from the Outside tileset. I came to a certain place where I needed the river to cascade over a cliff and continue at the bottom. Unfortunately, the the water + snow autotile in Outside-A1 appears to be frozen, or at least it differs enough in color that it did not look quite right to me butted up against the waterfall cliff tiles. I was able to crop out the water from the water + grass autotile in Outside-A1 and the stony embankment from the wwamp autotile in Outside-A2 and combine them to create an animated stony banked water autotile I could apply to any terrain autotile I needed.
